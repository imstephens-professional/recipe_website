| Egg in Toast | Alessandro Stephens | 1 serving | 5-10 minutes | Easy | <ul><li>1 tbsp butter</li> <li>1 tbsp olive oil</li> <li>1 tsp grated parmesan</li> <li>1/2 tsp black pepper</li> <li>Pinch of salt</li> <li>1/2 tsp oregano</li> <li>2 chicken eggs</li> <li>1 slice bread</li> | <ul><li>Cut a mid-sized hole in the bread.</li> <li>Discard the middle piece.</li> <li>Warm the butter and olive oil in a sautepan until it's bubbling.</li> <li>Crack open the eggs and fry them sunny-side up.</li> <li>While the eggs are frying, add the salt, black pepper, and oregano to the eggs.</li> <li>Cook thoroughly.</li> <li>Add the eggs to the center of the bread.</li> <li>Add grated parmesan on top.</li> |

## Beverages
| **Dish Name** | **Author's Name** | **Serving Size** | **Cooking Time** | **Difficulty** | **Ingredients** | **Steps** | 
| :----: | :----: | :----: | :----: | :----: | :----: | :----: | 
| Fruit Slush | Eva M. E. Green | 10 servings | 12 minutes | Easy | <ul><li>2 cups granulated sugar</li> <li>3 cups distilled water</li> <li>2 cups orange juice</li> <li>1/4 cups lemon juice</li> <li>14 oz can pineapple juice</li> <li>3 mashed bananas</li> <li>3 qt ginger ale</li> | <ul><li>Boil sugar and water until sugar dissolves.</li> <li>Cool the mixture.</li> <li>Add fruit juices and bananas.</li> <li>Chill. Do not completely freeze.</li> <li>Add ginger ale when serving.</li> |
| Orange Julius | Judith G. Allen | 5 servings | 5 minutes | Easy | <ul><li>1/2 can (6 oz) frozen orange juice concentrate</li> <li>1/2 cup milk</li> <li>1/4 cup sugar</li> <li>5 ice cubes</li> <li>1/2 cup water</li> <li>1 tsp vanilla extract</li> | <ul><li>Add orange juice concentrate, milk, sugar, water, and vanilla extract to a pitcher and mix together.</li> <li>Put the ice cubes in.</li> |
| French Chocolate | Eva M. E. Green | 8 servings | 10 minutes | Easy | <ul><li>2 1/2 qt unsweetened chocolate</li> <li>1 cup cold water</li> <li>3/4 cup granulated sugar</li> <li>1/2 tsp salt</li> <li>1/2 cup whipping cream</li> <li>whole milk</li> | <ul><li>Stir chocolate and water over direct heat for 4 minutes.</li> <li>Add sugar and salt to mixture.</li> <li>Stir for another 4 minutes over direct heat.</li> <li>Cool mixture.</li> <li>Add whipping cream.</li> <li>When ready to serve, heat the milk and add 1 large tablespoon or more of the chocolate mixture to each cup of hot milk.</li> |
