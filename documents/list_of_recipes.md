| Egg in Toast | Alessandro Stephens | 1 serving | 5-10 minutes | Easy | <ul><li>1 tbsp butter</li> <li>1 tbsp olive oil</li> <li>1 tsp grated parmesan</li> <li>1/2 tsp black pepper</li> <li>Pinch of salt</li> <li>1/2 tsp oregano</li> <li>2 chicken eggs</li> <li>1 slice bread</li> | <ul><li>Cut a mid-sized hole in the bread.</li> <li>Discard the middle piece.</li> <li>Warm the butter and olive oil in a sautepan until it's bubbling.</li> <li>Crack open the eggs and fry them sunny-side up.</li> <li>While the eggs are frying, add the salt, black pepper, and oregano to the eggs.</li> <li>Cook thoroughly.</li> <li>Add the eggs to the center of the bread.</li> <li>Add grated parmesan on top.</li> |
